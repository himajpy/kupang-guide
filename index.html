Discover Kupang

Welcome to Kupang, a beautiful city in Nusa Tenggara Timur! Here are some tips to make the most of your visit:

Places to Visit

  • Lasiana Beach
  • Kupang Waterfront
  • Oenesu Waterfall

Local Foods to Try

  • Kopi O' - everything
  • Jagung Bose - corn with coconut

Tips for Travelers

Always carry cash, as many places do not accept credit cards.
